MomentO

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 April 2026

MomentO is a social photo and games app for nights out, events, and gatherings. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled. We collect only what we need to run the app.

1. No account required

MomentO does not ask for your name, email address, or any personally identifying information. You are never asked to sign up or log in with a personal account.

2. Anonymous identity

When you open the app, Firebase Authentication creates an anonymous session for you. This session is linked to a random identifier stored on your device. It has no connection to your real identity. If you uninstall the app, the identifier is gone.

3. Session data

When you create or join a session, we store the following in our cloud database (Google Firestore):

  • A randomly generated session code
  • The event type you selected (e.g. games night, pub crawl)
  • The number of players in the session
  • Timestamps (when the session was created and last active)

Sessions expire after 48 hours and are not linked to any personal identity.

4. Photos

If you use the gallery feature, photos you upload are stored in Google Firebase Storage and are accessible to other participants in the same session. Photos are not shared outside your session. When a session ends, photos are retained temporarily before being deleted. You can delete your own photos from within the app at any time.

5. Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Whether a session was created or joined
  • Which games were played and whether they were completed
  • Whether photos were captured
  • Whether users return to the app

This data is anonymous and is used only to understand how the app is being used so we can improve it. It is not sold or used for advertising.

6. Crash reporting

We use Sentry to collect crash reports. When the app crashes, Sentry captures technical information such as the error, the device type, and the OS version. This helps us fix bugs. No personal information is included in crash reports.

7. Third-party services

The following third-party services process data as part of running MomentO:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage) — Privacy policy
  • PostHog (Analytics) — Privacy policy
  • Sentry (Crash reporting) — Privacy policy

8. Data storage and security

Data is stored on Google's infrastructure in accordance with Google's security standards. We do not store payment information. We do not maintain user profiles. We do not have access to the contents of your photos beyond what Firebase Storage logs for operational purposes.

9. Children's privacy

MomentO is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will delete the associated data.

10. Your rights

Because we do not collect personal information tied to your identity, we are unable to retrieve or delete data on your behalf by name or email. Uninstalling the app removes your anonymous device identifier. Any session data you created will expire naturally within 48 hours.

If you are in the EU or UK, you have rights under GDPR / UK GDPR. Because we collect only anonymous, non-identifying data, most GDPR rights (access, rectification, portability) do not apply in a meaningful way. For any questions, contact us below.

11. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
